Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about Aura Fragrances.

How long do the scents last?

expand_more

Our fragrances are Extrait de Parfum concentration, containing 25-30% perfume oil, ensuring a longevity of 8-12 hours on skin and even longer on fabrics.

Are your fragrances vegan and cruelty-free?

expand_more

Yes, all Aura fragrances are 100% vegan and never tested on animals. We prioritize ethical sourcing for all our ingredients.

Do you offer international shipping?

expand_more

We currently ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping times vary by location but typically arrive within 5-10 business days.
